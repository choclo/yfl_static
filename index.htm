to log your flights

Online flight–log book software for Paraglider and Hang-glider pilots

Get Started Now, it's free!

Your old paper log-book isn't all that great. Sorry.

No more loose pieces of paper stuffed inside gear bags, blown away by a gust of wind, lost while traveling, or soaked by leaking water bottles

It's soo quick and easy

Easily log your flights, tracking launch types, conditions, equipment usage and more

  • View flights plotted on maps
  • Automatic calculation of equipment airtime use
  • Easy data-entry for wind-direction, launch types, flight types, etc
  • Attach photos and videos from your flights (coming soon)
  • Share flights with your friends with Facebook integration (coming soon)

Map your flights and flying sites

What better way to view your flights than by on the map?

  • Map your own flying sites
  • Discover other flying sites around your area
  • Upload GPS track logs of individual flights (coming soon)
  • View GPS track logs directly on your map (coming soon)

Track your equipment

Keep a track of all your equipment in one place

  • Track all your aircraft and equipment types
  • Automatically calculate air-time use, so you'll know when your gear needs professional servicing, so you can fly with confidence
  • Store serial numbers and photos of your equipment for insurance purposes

We'll keep your log book safe

Losing your log book can be a real hassle. Keep it safe with us. With YourFlightLog you won't have to worry. Let us securely store and backup your data. You can access your flight log online at anytime

Print, export… it's your data!

It's your data, so we provide a number of ways for you to export your flight log. Export your flight log as a CSV, Excel spreadsheet, or a PDF.

Mobile support

(Coming soon) Record your flights while sitting on the hill. Your current flying site's location will be automagically selected for you.

How It Works

Fly

Grab your gear and go flying. Be sure to track your flight time with a watch or a variometer/gps device.

Log

Use your recorded flight time, location, wind (optional) and the gear you used to log your flight.

Done

That's all! Now you can see the list of flights you have done as well as locations, duration, total flight hours and more!

Join to YourFlightLog, it's free Fly. Log. Done.

About us
YourFlightLog is being developed by free-flight enthusiasts. We strive to give pilots around the world the best experience and product for logging their flight hours, locations, equipment and more. This site and app are using the latest programming and design techniques, with ultra-fast servers and security in mind. We invite all pilots to use our application and give us feedback through email or the feedback link within the app.
